PROJECTS

Throughout my career, I have worked on numerous projects that have honed my skills as a software engineer. Here are a few of the notable ones:

Farm-Door

As a software developer, I contributed to the development of the Farm-Door web and mobile application, an innovative agricultural commerce service. This platform bridges the gap between farmers and buyers, providing farmers with a dedicated space to market and sell their products while offering buyers an accessible marketplace for purchasing agricultural goods. Additionally, the application features a profile login for users seeking employment as casual laborers, facilitating the hiring process within the agricultural sector. This project highlights my ability to create user-centric solutions that address the needs of diverse user groups within the agricultural community.

OpHub

In my role as a software developer, I also worked on the Opportunity Hub platform, a dynamic solution designed to support innovators at any stage of their projects. This platform serves as a showcase for innovative ideas, allowing creators to present their work to potential investors, including government entities and stakeholders. By providing a space for innovators to gain visibility and secure funding, Opportunity Hub plays a crucial role in fostering innovation and development. My involvement in this project underscores my commitment to leveraging technology to create impactful platforms that empower users and drive progress.
